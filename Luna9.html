Luna 9


Luna 9, launched by the Soviet Union on January 31, 1966, was the first spacecraft to successfully land on the Moon and transmit photographs back to Earth. It provided the first clear images of the lunar surface, showing its rocky terrain and confirming the feasibility of soft landings on other celestial bodies.
