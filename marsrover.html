Curiosity Rover


Curiosity Rover, launched on November 26, 2011, and landing on Mars on August 6, 2012, is part of NASA's Mars Science Laboratory mission. It has been exploring Gale Crater, analyzing rock samples, and studying the planet's climate and geology. Curiosity’s findings, including evidence of ancient water, organic molecules, and seasonal methane variations, have significantly advanced our understanding of Mars’ potential for habitability and laid the groundwork for future human missions..
