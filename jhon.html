Friendship 7


John Glenn's Friendship 7 mission, launched on February 20, 1962, marked him as the first American to orbit Earth. As part of NASA's Mercury program, the mission's successful flight made Glenn an international hero. He orbited Earth three times, and the mission provided valuable data on human spaceflight.
