Pioneer 10


Pioneer 10, launched on March 2, 1972, was the first spacecraft to travel beyond the asteroid belt and into interstellar space. It provided valuable data on Jupiter’s atmosphere, magnetosphere, and radiation belts. Pioneer 10’s successful journey marked the beginning of humanity’s exploration beyond the solar system, and it continues to be a symbol of human achievement in space exploration.
