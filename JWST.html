James Web Telescope


The James Webb Space Telescope (JWST), launched on December 25, 2021, is the most powerful space telescope ever built. Positioned at the second Lagrange point (L2), JWST observes the universe in infrared, allowing it to peer through dust clouds and observe distant galaxies, star formation, and exoplanet atmospheres. Its unprecedented resolution and sensitivity are providing groundbreaking insights into the early universe, cosmic evolution, and the potential for life beyond Earth. here we can see one of the most famous photos taken by the JWST
